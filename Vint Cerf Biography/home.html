Vint Cerf

"The Father of Intertnet"

Cerf known as a “Father of the Internet”, Cerf is the co-designer of the TCP/IP protocols and the architecture of the Internet. Some would argue that Joseph Carl Roberts Licklider created the Internet. However, he created time-sharing, and the person who created TCP and IP is Vinton “Vint” Cerf.
